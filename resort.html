Welcome to Nature

Where the Forest Breathes

Nestled amidst the lush coffee plantations and dense rainforests of Coorg, Stanley's Resort is a haven for those seeking solitude and serenity. We offer an immersive experience where the only sounds you hear are the chirping of exotic birds and the rustle of leaves.

Our resort is designed to blend seamlessly with the environment, offering luxury that doesn't compromise the beauty of nature. Wake up to the aroma of fresh coffee and misty mountains right outside your window.
